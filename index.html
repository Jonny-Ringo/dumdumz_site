Wat iz DumDumz?

DumDumz is the OG collection of AO and Arweave, with 1,984 pieces there is a Dumz for everyone!

Wat iz Dumverse?

Dumverse is a 100% on-chain, turn-based PvP adventure game, powered by $DUMZ. DumDumz holders can step into the game as their character, unlocking exclusive in-game benefits and perks. Don't have a DumDumz? Play as a base DumDumz and join the adventure with your fellow Dumz!
